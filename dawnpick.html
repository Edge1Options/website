Buy Example: Tesla

If Tesla closes at $250 and you predict it will rise, you buy 1 CFD contract (equivalent to 1 share) at $250. This requires a margin of $25 USDe (10% leverage).

Deposit margin according to the leverage ratio (10% = 10x leverage)

Leverage amplifies both profits and losses. Please manage your position size carefully.

Hold the contract until automatic settlement before next market open, or close manually anytime
Profit or loss is calculated based on the difference between closing and opening prices

Trading stops 15 minutes after market close and resumes 30 minutes before next open

Settlement Example

If Tesla opens at $260 the next day, your profit for a long position would be:
($260 - $250) × 1 share = $10 USDe (40% return)
If the price drops to $240, your loss would be:
($250 - $240) × 1 share = $10 USDe (40% loss)

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about CFD trading

Decentralized CFD trading refers to差价合约交易 implemented through blockchain smart contracts, eliminating the need for a centralized platform to hold funds. On the DawnPick platform, all trading logic is automatically executed by smart contracts. User funds are stored in their own wallets and only interact with the platform via smart contracts during trades, greatly enhancing fund security.

CFD trading involves significant risks, including:
1. Leverage Risk: 10x leverage can amplify losses by 10 times
2. Market Risk: Underlying asset price fluctuations may result in losses
3. Liquidity Risk: Difficulty closing positions during extreme market conditions
Recommendation: Start with small trades, set stop-loss orders, and limit position size to no more than 20% of total capital.

Profit/Loss Calculation Formulas:
Long Position Profit = (Closing Price - Opening Price) × Contract Quantity
Short Position Profit = (Opening Price - Closing Price) × Contract Quantity

Example: Going long 1 Tesla CFD at $250 and closing at $260 yields (260-250)×1=$10 profit. If the closing price is $240, the loss is $10.

USDe is a stablecoin issued on the ICP blockchain, pegged 1:1 to the US dollar. During the test phase, users can obtain USDe through the following methods:
1. Receive a test allocation after registration (500 USDe)
2. Invite friends to earn rewards (100 USDe per successful referral)
3. Participate in platform activities

After the mainnet launch, USDe can be obtained by exchanging ICP or transferring from other major stablecoins via cross-chain bridges.

DawnPick ensures fund security through the following measures:
1. Smart contracts audited by both CertiK and SlowMist
2. Funds stored in user wallets, platform has no direct access
3. On-chain transactions are transparent and traceable
4. "Emergency pause" mechanism for extreme market conditions
5. Cold/hot wallet separation, majority of funds stored in cold wallets
