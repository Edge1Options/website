Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Edge1 and options trading.

Options are financial derivatives that give the holder the right, but not the obligation, to buy or sell an asset at a specific price in the future. They provide flexible investment choices for strategic trading.

Edge1 uses advanced encryption and smart contract technology, undergoing rigorous security audits to protect user assets and transaction data in a decentralized environment.

Edge1 is fully decentralized on ICP with professional financial pricing models, avoiding simplifications seen in other ecosystems, and offers a complete options product matrix.

ICP offers exceptional computing power and performance for complex financial models, with a rapidly growing ecosystem aligned with Edge1's vision of open, decentralized finance.

Edge1 supports European, American, Barrier, and Binary options, with plans to continuously expand our product line to meet diverse trading strategies.
